# KING OF BATTLE - COLOSSEO ARENA

**Concept:** TikTok Live-ready 9:16 gladiator tournament in a Roman Colosseum. Viewers type JOIN to enter as stickman fighters.

**Core mechanic:** Auto-combat 1v1 tournament — fighters attack every 2-3s with random damage (12-25HP). Winner stays, loser is replaced by next in queue (capsule waiting area). Donated viewers unlock special moves via action buttons.

**Art style:** Dark gothic Hollow Arena aesthetic — deep purple/black palette, glowing rune borders, fighter portrait panels at top, gothic dark background. Stylized stickman gladiators with capes, colored auras, and swords drawn on canvas. AI gothic background asset.
